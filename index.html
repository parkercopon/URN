About me:

Hello, I'm from a snowy place in the USA. (Not Alaska). Someday I want to move to the Netherlands, or Poland.
I'm an artist and a big fan of Furry Characters! Two of my favorites are the pink fox thing from FNAF and the dusty blue wolf from Beastars, I think they should go on a date. They probably don't have much in common but I bet they'd be super anxious trying to make each other happy, and not really understanding each other. It'd probably end really badly, and theyd cry and fight a lot. Um uhh... I mean, food for thought, y'know.
Other than that? Well... I play piano. I guess that's not too important. I'm currently learning Moonlight Sonata; I have parts 1 and 2 down, but I'm working on Presto Agitato.
