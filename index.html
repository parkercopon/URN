WHAT AM I LOOKING AT?

Hello! This is my website. I wanted to make a website because it's a convenient place to show off my art.
What is my art? Simple! It's Pixel Art!
"Pixel Art" has existed for thousands of years, in many forms. Looms, tiling, and knitted sweaters are all examples of the same kind of art. I use computer pixels!

There is other stuff here too. Enjoy! Thank you!

CLOSING THOUGHTS

Sometimes in my life I feel like the smartest coolest person the world, or like I'm living in my own world that was built for me. I can become arrogant and waste your time (I am sorry if we meet and I do this to you). Remember to take a step back and think about the people outside of your own life. Always keep living!! Live with urgency and gusto and full of love for everything. Never be arrogant; there is no one to be arrogant to. Think hard about your thinking, and you will not go wrong. Go! With love!!<3
As I'm writing this I'm 19. I still have a long time to live, yet my life is so short. "Such is the human experience..." The connections you make, the friends you find, the love you share, will outlive you. It's our legacy. Always love people.
